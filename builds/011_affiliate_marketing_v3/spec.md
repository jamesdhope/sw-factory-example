# Technical Specification for Affiliate Blog Post: Best Zero-Gravity Chairs for Back Pain 2024

## Overview
To create a comprehensive and monetizable blog post titled 'Best Zero-Gravity Chairs for Back Pain 2024', this specification outlines the required components and modules. The blog post will focus on product recommendations to help alleviate back pain through the use of zero-gravity chairs.

## Modules

### 1. Market Research
- Conduct detailed market analysis to identify top-performing zero-gravity chairs as of 2024.
- Evaluate ergonomics, customer reviews, and pricing.
- Compile a list of at least 10 chairs to be included in the blog post.

### 2. SEO Copywriting
- Develop content that targets high-traffic keywords related to zero-gravity chairs and back pain relief.
- Include meta descriptions and appropriate headings (H1, H2, etc.).
- Structure content to be engaging and informative.

### 3. Monetization Link Injection
- Integrate affiliate links seamlessly within the blog content.
- Ensure links lead to reputable retail platforms offering affiliate programs.
- Maintain transparency with readers by providing an affiliate disclosure.

## Additional Features
- Implement a 5-star rating system for each recommended product, clearly displayed alongside each chair review.
- Add an affiliate disclosure section at the beginning or end of the post to inform readers of the affiliate nature of the links.

## Deliverables
- A draft of the affiliate blog post following the specified structure and including all required components.
- SEO-optimized content ready for publication.
- Successfully embedded affiliate links in compliance with platform guidelines and best practices.
